Research and Development Quality Assurance Process

Peer Reviews

While we are in the research phase of the redesigning UpStage project, our main tool for ensuring quality will be peer reviews. Upon or nearing completion, a member of the team will be assigned to inspect and review plans, designs, prototypes or code. If rating the quality of something in particular is unclear to the team, the supervisor will be contacted for help on determining this. Examples of this can be seen in our Motivational document which has been revised by multiple members, as well as our supervisor which include comments. we have also used the peer review process with the technology matrix for our proof of concept.
