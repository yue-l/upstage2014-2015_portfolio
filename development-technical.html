Reflection

On reflection we discovered that the team members were struggling to fully complete a bug fix in a week. Therefore we took this into consideration when devising our new plan and decided to have two bug fixes done fortnightly. We have contacted our client in order to determine which are the most immediately important bugs to fix which we plan to start with. We also plan to have a uniform way to communicate effectively to our clients and future developers what we discovered in the process, as well as the progress on the fix we made
